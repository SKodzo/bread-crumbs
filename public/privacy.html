1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Village Collective LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Bread Crumbs mobile application and associated website ("Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Bread Crumbs is designed to help prospective homebuyers understand their financial readiness, explore mortgage options, and identify down payment assistance programs. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about what data we handle and why.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide

  • Account credentials: Email address and password (for account creation and login).
  • Optional contact info: Phone number (optional, provided during signup).
  • Financial planning inputs: Income, household size, estimated monthly expenses, target home price, credit score range, down payment percentage, debt obligations, and savings balances. These are entered voluntarily to generate your personalized homebuying roadmap.
  • Location (ZIP code): Used to look up local property tax rates, insurance estimates, utility averages, and available down payment assistance programs for your area.

2b. Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data: Which screens you visit and how far you've progressed in the planning flow (stored as part of your saved progress).
  • Device information: Operating system version and device type, collected by our infrastructure provider for security and performance purposes.

2c. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not access your bank accounts, credit reports, or any financial accounts.
  • We do not collect precise GPS location — only the ZIP code you enter manually.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not collect biometric data of any kind.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the Bread Crumbs Services:

  • To authenticate your account and save your progress across sessions.
  • To calculate personalized mortgage estimates, debt-to-income ratios, and affordability ranges based on your inputs.
  • To identify down payment assistance programs for which you may be eligible based on your income, household size, profession, and location.
  • To retrieve area-specific data (FHA loan limits, area median income) from public government APIs on your behalf.
  • To send you a password reset email if you request one.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the security of our Services.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing you with homebuying planning tools.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account and financial planning data are stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database platform. Key security measures include:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS (HTTPS) encryption.
  • Encryption at rest: Your data is encrypted at rest within Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database-level access controls ensure you can only access your own data — our servers cannot inadvertently serve your data to another user.
  • No plain-text passwords: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt algorithms and are never stored or accessible in plain text.
  • Secure storage on-device: Session tokens and acknowledgment flags are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Secure Enclave / Android Keystore) via Expo SecureStore.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database), Vercel (API hosting), and Expo (app build and distribution). These providers process data only as necessary to deliver our Services and are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.
  • Public APIs: To look up FHA loan limits and area median income for your location, we query public U.S. government APIs (HUD, FRED). Only your state and county are transmitted — no personally identifiable information is sent.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the safety and legal rights of our users or the public.
  • Business transfer: If Village Collective LLC is acquired or merged, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users via email and in-app notice before any such transfer occurs and describe any material changes to this Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

Bread Crumbs integrates with or relies upon the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — Authentication and database storage. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — Serverless API hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) — Public API for FHA loan limits and Area Median Income data.
  • Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis (FRED) — Public API for current mortgage rate benchmarks.
  • Expo / EAS (expo.dev) — App build and over-the-air update infrastructure. Subject to Expo's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their policies.

7. Financial Information Disclaimer

Important: Bread Crumbs is an educational planning tool, not a licensed financial, mortgage, or legal services provider. Nothing in this application constitutes financial advice, a mortgage pre-approval, or a guarantee of program eligibility.

All calculations, estimates, and program eligibility determinations are based on publicly available data and information you provide. They are approximations only. Actual loan terms, qualification outcomes, tax obligations, and program availability will vary based on your specific circumstances, lender underwriting requirements, and current market conditions.

Always consult with a licensed HUD-approved housing counselor, mortgage lender, financial advisor, or real estate attorney before making any financial decision.

The financial inputs you enter into Bread Crumbs are used solely to power the app's calculations. They are not transmitted to lenders, credit bureaus, government agencies, or any financial institution without your explicit consent and action.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information and planning data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the in-app account deletion feature, your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems, including:

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • All saved financial planning inputs and progress
  • Any associated profile data

Deletion is processed immediately upon your request. Backup systems may retain data for up to 30 days as part of standard database backup procedures, after which it is purged entirely.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update your email address or password through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data at any time using the "Delete account" option within the app, or by contacting us at the address below.
  • Portability: You may request an export of your saved financial planning data in a structured format.
  • Opt-out: Since we do not sell data or send marketing emails, there is no marketing opt-out required. You may disable in-app notifications through your device's notification settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at breadcrumbsfina@gmail.com. We will respond as soon as reasonably possible.

10. Children's Privacy

Bread Crumbs is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a user under 18 has created an account, we will promptly delete their information. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You may request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell).
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To submit a verifiable consumer request, email us at breadcrumbsfina@gmail.com. We will verify your identity before processing the request.

12. International Users (GDPR)

Bread Crumbs is primarily designed for U.S. homebuyers. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability.

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent (provided at account creation) and our legitimate interest in providing the Services you requested.

Data is stored on servers located in the United States. By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your data to the U.S. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at breadcrumbsfina@gmail.com.
